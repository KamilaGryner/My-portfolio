About Me

My name is Kamila Gryner and I am a programming enthusiast from Cracow. On a daily basis, I teach programming to young people as a trainer at Giants of Programming, and I also teach computer science and computer graphics at ZSE Technical School No. 9. I have also been working as a freelancer since 2024, creating websites and computer games.


I graduated with a degree in engineering and a master's degree in technical and computer science education from AGH University of Science and Technology in Cracow, with grades of 4.5 and 5.0 respectively.


I have experience working with many technologies, including: JavaScript, Python, C++, HTML/CSS, React, Angular, Firebase, as well as in team projects ranging from website and game development to participation in scientific conferences. I have previously worked with the AGH Solar Boat team, among others, and participated in international competitions and presentations.


I am a communicative person who enjoys teamwork and development. I love sharing knowledge - that's why others often ask me to teach them something.
